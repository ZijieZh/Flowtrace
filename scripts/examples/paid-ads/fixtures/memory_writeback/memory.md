## Week 7 — decision

**Binding constraint (Theory of Constraints):** hero-video creative fatigue in Meta prospecting —
the 0:30 hero ran unchanged 5 weeks; CTR −50% as frequency hit 4.8 (+55%), dragging blended CPA to
$71 (+22%) and ROAS to 2.6x.

**Chosen this week:**
- **H1** — ship a NEW long-story video built on the before/after sleep-score structure (applies wk6
  pattern + wk2 format) vs. the fatigued hero. 50/50, primary metric prospecting CTR, ~10–14 days.
- **H2** — frequency cap ~3/wk and move LAL-3% budget into LAL-1% (delivery fix, isolated from H1).
- Launched **Wed 9:00 ET** (wk3). Free-shipping end card (wk5), not a discount.

**Explicitly skipped (memory ruled these out — recorded so we don't relitigate):**
- ✗ Short punchy-hook videos — **wk2** says long-story wins ~1.8x.
- ✗ 15–20% off to match Competitor A / Competitor B — **wk5** retired %-off; free-shipping framing wins.
- ✗ Defending the hot-sleeper "cooling" angle — seasonal + contested + transient; not the constraint.

**Parked for week 8:** H3 — route more gift-intent to the quiz funnel (wk4); deferred to avoid
confounding H1's creative read.
