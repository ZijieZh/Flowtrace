# Week 7 Test Variants — H1: fresh long-story video vs. fatigued hero

**Single variable under test:** the prospecting video creative. Everything else (audience LAL-1%,
placement, bid, landing path) is held constant so the read is clean.

**Treatment angle** (from ad-creative's angle table): **Outcome + Identity** — "wake up to a measurably
better sleep score," built on the brand's proven fatigue-resistant *before/after sleep-score* structure
(memory wk6), told as a long-story cut (memory wk2: long-story beats short hooks ~1.8x). This is NOT a
new snappy hook — that approach is retired for this audience.

---

## A. Meta — prospecting video ad

### Control (current — fatigued)
- **Creative:** 0:30 "unboxing → first night" hero video (live unchanged 5 weeks).
- **Primary text:** "The duvet that feels like a hug. Sleep deeper from night one." (61 chars)
- **Headline:** "Meet the Weighted Silk Duvet" (28 chars)

### Treatment (new — on-pattern)
- **Creative:** 0:35 long-story cut: real customer, week-1 vs. week-3 sleep-score cards on screen
  (before/after structure), free-shipping end card (memory wk5: free-shipping framing, not %-off).
- **Primary text** *(Meta: 125 visible, front-load the hook)*:
  1. "Her sleep score went from 61 to 84 in three weeks. Same bed. New duvet." (71 chars) ✓
  2. "Three weeks. One change. Sleep score 61 → 84. Free shipping, 100-night trial." (77 chars) ✓
- **Headline** *(Meta: ≤40 recommended)*:
  1. "From restless to an 84 sleep score" (34 chars) ✓
  2. "A measurably better night, from night one" (41 chars) → trim → "Measurably better sleep, night one" (34 chars) ✓
- **Description** *(Meta: ≤30 recommended)*:
  1. "Free shipping · 100-night trial" (31 chars) → trim → "Free shipping, 100-night trial" (30 chars) ✓
